3-D optical hash + energy minimisation theory

OpticalHash3D — sparse voxel hash over (x,y,λ) space.

Hash function: h(ix,iy,iλ) = (ix·p₁ ⊕ iy·p₂ ⊕ iλ·p₃) mod N (polynomial XOR)

Energy functional:

H = 0.1·H_TV + 0.4·H_pc

H_TV = ½·Σ|E_i − E_j|² — total variation (penalises abrupt field changes)

H_pc = Σ(|E_k|−1)² — TD-GS pure-phase constraint

Gradient descent drives the stored field toward a smooth unit-amplitude wavefront.

OpticalLSH — locality-sensitive hashing for O(1) approximate nearest-neighbour retrieval. Random projections: h(v)=sign(Av+b)∈{0,1}^n

Plots: wavefront amplitude scatter · |E|(λ) scatter · hash bucket histogram · energy convergence (H_TV, H_pc, H_total) · (x,λ) projection
